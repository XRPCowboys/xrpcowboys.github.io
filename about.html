Who We Are

The XRPCowboys Story

XRPCowboys was founded in 2018 as a grassroots community for XRP enthusiasts. What started as informal discussions among early holders has grown into a respected, members-only forum dedicated to the XRP Ledger and its real-world adoption.

Today, XRPCowboys is operated by Twin BlockChain Technologies LLC and serves as a private space where verified members connect, share insights, attend virtual events, and build lasting relationships. A fun tradition in our community is wearing cowboy hats at conferences and meetups — a symbol of the pioneering spirit we bring to the XRP ecosystem.

We provide a private forum-style platform where verified members discuss XRP technology, ecosystem developments, regulatory updates, investment strategies, and real-world adoption of the XRP Ledger. Members connect directly with industry leaders, developers, and high-net-worth holders in a moderated, professional environment.
