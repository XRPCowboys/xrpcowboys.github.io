✦ Online Forum & Discussion Platform
XRPCowboys — HorsePower Wire
XRPCowboys is a private, members-only online forum and community founded in 2018 for serious participants in the XRP Ledger ecosystem. Through our HorsePower Wire discussion platform, verified members engage in thoughtful conversations about XRP technology, ecosystem developments, regulatory updates, real-world adoption, and industry events.

XRPCowboys is a private, members-only online forum and community founded in 2018 for serious participants in the XRP Ledger ecosystem.

Through our HorsePower Wire discussion platform, verified members engage in thoughtful conversations about XRP technology, ecosystem developments, regulatory updates, real-world adoption, and industry events.

A fun tradition in our community is wearing cowboy hats at conferences and meetups — a symbol of the pioneering, independent spirit we bring to the XRP space.

Operated by Twin BlockChain Technologies LLC, XRPCowboys serves as the official home for the XRP Cowboys community. Membership is selective and by application only.

2018

The Beginning

XRPCowboys founded by a small group of early XRP holders and believers. The cowboy hat tradition begins at our first meetup.

2019

Community Grows

Membership expands as the XRP community rallies together through market cycles. First private online forum launched.

2020

Holding Strong

Through the SEC lawsuit and market volatility, XRPCowboys members hold firm and support one another through the storm via our online forum.

2022

Twin BlockChain Technologies LLC

XRPCowboys formally incorporated under Twin BlockChain Technologies LLC, establishing official operations and governance of the online forum platform.

2025

Expansion & Events

XRPCowboys hosts and attends major XRP events including Swell, XRP Las Vegas, and community parties worldwide. The HorsePower Wire forum grows to hundreds of active members.

2026

Today

XRPCowboys continues to grow as one of the most respected private online forum communities in the XRP ecosystem. Membership by application only.

Today, XRPCowboys continues to serve as the dedicated online gathering place for the XRP Cowboys community — connecting holders, builders, and enthusiasts through active forum discussions and member events.
