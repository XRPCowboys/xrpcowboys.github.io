Vegas Baby —
XRP Takes The Strip

April 18, 2026 • XRPCowboys Editorial • 11 Days Away
← Back to The Corral

The XRP community is heading to Las Vegas — and this time, we're not just watching from the sidelines. April 30 through May 1, 2026, the Strip becomes home to one of the most anticipated crypto gatherings of the year, and XRPCowboys will be riding right into the heart of it.

Why Vegas, Why Now

After years of regulatory uncertainty, courtroom drama, and market volatility, the XRP community has earned its victory lap. With legal clarity finally on the horizon and institutional adoption accelerating, there's never been a better time to gather, celebrate, and strategize for what comes next.

"We've been riding through storms that would've broken most. Vegas isn't just a party — it's a statement." — Dave Goodman, Co-Founder

What to Expect

Two days of panels, networking, and community events featuring some of the biggest names in the XRP ecosystem. Whether you're a long-time hodler, a developer building on the XRPL, or just curious about what this community is all about — there's a seat at the table for you.

The Posse Rides Together

XRPCowboys will have a presence throughout the event. Look for our crew, connect with fellow members, and join us for exclusive side events you won't find on any official agenda. Details drop closer to the date — make sure you're in the Corral when they do.

Las Vegas, April 30. See you there, cowboy. 🤠
